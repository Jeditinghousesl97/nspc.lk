Our Firm

Accounting, Audit, Tax & Advisory Professionals

NS Perera Company is an independent body of accountants and auditors providing accounting, audit, tax and advisory services for businesses in Sri Lanka.

Our work is based on professionalism, integrity and specialized knowledge. We focus on added-value services that protect client interests while maintaining the highest ethical standards.

Mission

To provide reliable professional services with integrity, excellence and confidentiality to meet each customer’s individual requirements.


Vision

To be an employee-friendly chartered accountant firm rendering a full range of quality professional services while abiding by strict ethical and moral principles.
